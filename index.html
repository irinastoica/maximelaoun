About Me

Who I Am?

My name is Maxime Laoun, I am young athlete born and raised in Montreal.

I began my golf career at the young age of one and half year old, when my father first showed me how to hit balls with small plastic clubs. I graduated to my first real golf bag at the age of four and I started officially competing at the age of six. My hard work and dedication began to pay off when five years ago, I won the Quebec Juvenile Championship.

However, this coming year, I decided to concentrate more on my other passion, short track speed skating, my sights set on qualifying for the Olympics. I still currently have the chance to practice my golfing skills at the most prestigious golf course in Canada, The Royal Montreal, when I get a break from skating. So, if one day, I want to return to a competitive level in golf, which is what I expect, I will be able to.
